🐺 Wolf Cup Admin Guide

For the League Commissioner — wolf.dagle.cloud/admin

1. Logging In

1 Go to wolf.dagle.cloud/admin
2 Enter the admin password and tap Login
You stay logged in on your device. You shouldn't need to log in again unless you clear your browser data.

2. Season Setup (Once Per Year)

Do this before the first round of the season.

Create the Season

Admin → Season

1 Tap Create Season
2 Set the name (e.g., "2026 Wolf Cup Season")
3 Set start date (first Friday) and end date (last Friday)
4 The system auto-creates a round for every Friday in that range with rotating tees (Black → Blue → White → repeat)
After creation, you'll see all Fridays listed with checkboxes. Uncheck any weeks that won't be played (e.g., Member-Guest week). You can also change the tee for any specific week.

Update the Roster

Admin → Roster

1 Add any new players — enter their name and GHIN number if they have one
2 Tap a player's status badge to cycle: Active → Sub → Inactive. Active and Sub players show in attendance; Inactive ones are hidden.
3 Tap Refresh All Handicaps to pull latest GHIN indexes for everyone

3. Running a Friday Round

This is your weekly routine on game day.

Before the Round — Take Attendance

Attend tab (visible to everyone, but only admin can change it)

The Attend page shows the upcoming Friday automatically — week number, date, and tees.

1 Every active roster player is listed with their handicap index. Tap each name to toggle them in (green dot) or out (red dot).
2 Work through the list based on the GroupMe chat — green for confirmed, red for out.
3 Need a sub who isn't on the roster? Scroll to the bottom and tap Add Sub. Search by name or GHIN number.
4 When attendance is set, scroll to the bottom and tap Create Round. This activates the round with the join code (the year: 2026).
The confirmed count shows at the top (e.g., "8/19 confirmed"). Player count must be a multiple of 4 — groups of 4 only. If you have 10, either find 2 more or sit 2 out.

Players Join

Tell the group:

1 Go to wolf.dagle.cloud on your phone
2 Tap Score in the bottom nav
3 Enter the join code: 2026
4 Select your name and which group you're in

One person per group runs the scoring — they'll do the ball draw and enter scores hole by hole.

Ball Draw & Scoring

The scorer for each group handles this:

1 Ball Draw — add the 4 players, optionally shuffle the order with "Roll for Order", confirm
2 Score each hole — enter gross scores for all 4 players, then choose the wolf decision (partner, alone, blind wolf)
3 Mark greenies (par 3s) and polies (wolf holes 3+) when applicable
The leaderboard updates live as scores come in. Anyone can watch from the Board tab — no login needed.

After the Round — Finalize

Admin → Rounds → [the round]

1 Wait until all groups have finished hole 18
2 Review the leaderboard — make sure scores look right
3 Tap Finalize Round
Finalization locks scores and computes Harvey Cup points. Only do this when you're confident the scores are correct. You can still make score corrections after finalizing.

4. Score Corrections

Admin → Score Corrections

If someone reports a wrong score after finalization:

1 Select the round and group
2 Find the hole and player with the wrong score
3 Enter the correct gross score and a brief reason
4 The system automatically recalculates all money, stableford, and Harvey points for the entire group
Every correction is logged with a timestamp, old score, new score, and reason. Full audit trail.

5. Photos

Anyone can upload photos by tapping the camera icon in the top header bar.

  • Photos taken during an active round are automatically tagged to that round
  • You can upload multiple photos at once from your photo library
  • Photos appear in the gallery grouped by round

7. Troubleshooting

Player can't join

  • Make sure the round is active (not just scheduled)
  • Confirm they're using the right entry code (this year: 2026)
  • They may need to hard refresh their browser (pull down on mobile)

Wrong score entered

  • During the round: the scorer can go back to any hole and change it
  • After finalization: use Score Corrections in admin

Need to cancel a round

  • Admin → Rounds → the round → Cancel
  • Cancelled rounds don't count for standings or Harvey Cup

Handicaps look wrong

  • Handicap indexes are pulled from GHIN. Tap Refresh All Handicaps on the Roster page to get the latest.
  • Handicaps are snapshotted when the round is created — a mid-round GHIN change won't affect an active round.
