From The Cart
Score the hole in four taps
One scorer per foursome taps in the four gross numbers. Net, best-ball and money compute the second you save — no rebuilding the round on 18.
  • Big, fast number pads — tap and it advances to the next man
  • Teams & bonuses (greenie, polie, sandie) right on the card
  • Works offline — it syncs the moment you're back in range
Instant Results
The board moves the second you save
To-par and money for the whole field, live — no login to watch.
  • To-par & dollars per man — sorted, ranked, color-coded
  • Handicap index & course handicap right under each name
  • Tap any name to open their full hole-by-hole card
Hole By Hole
Every hole, settled as you go
Tap any player and their whole card opens — every hole color-coded, with net and money right there.
  • Color-coded scores — birdies, bogeys & blow-ups at a glance
  • Net & money per hole — no calculator, no arguments
  • Greenie, polie & sandie dots right on the card
The Money
Who owes who — done.
Every game's money is tracked live and rolled into one simple settle-up at the end. No spreadsheet, no "I think you owe me."
  • Standings in dollars — what each man is up or down
  • Head-to-head grid — exactly who's up on who
  • One end-of-trip number that nets everybody out
Johnny Hotdog's down $165 and still pressing. The app keeps the receipts — settle up like gentlemen.
Fair Play
Lock in handicaps by date
The Pete Dye Invitational only works if the strokes are fair. The organizer freezes every index as of a date you pick — posted, identical for everybody. No sandbagging week-of.
