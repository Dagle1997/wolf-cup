A 60-SECOND GUIDE

How to use
the app.

Everything you do this weekend, on your phone — join, score the hole, watch the board move, and call the action. Four screens, that's it.

1Join the event
2Score the hole
3Watch the leaderboard
4Post a bet — The Action
tournament.dagle.cloud
Open it in your phone's browser, then "Add to Home Screen".
Step 1

Join the event

No password, no sign-up. Tap the link Josh sends and pick your name — the app remembers you on that phone.

1

Open the invite link

Tap the link in the group text (or enter your join code at the app).

2

Tap your name

Find yourself in the roster and tap it — that binds you to your phone.

3

You're in

You land on the event home. Didn't get a link? Text Josh.

Step 2

Score the hole

One scorer per foursome enters everyone's gross score, hole by hole. Net, best-ball and money calculate themselves.

1

Tap the score

Type a one-digit score (it jumps to the next player), or use – / +. Bigger numbers: tap +.

2

Bonuses (if you play them)

Tap G / P / S for greenie, polie, sandie on the hole.

3

Save the hole

Hit Save Hole — it syncs and moves on. Works even with no signal; it catches up later.

Step 3

Watch the leaderboard

The board moves the second a score is saved. To-par and money, per man, live.

1

Open Leaderboard

From the event home, or the tab up top. Tap a player to open their card.

2

This round or the whole trip

Toggle Current round / All rounds. See To Par and $.

3

Jump around

Tap Score → to enter, or Foursome results → for the hole-by-hole card.

Step 4

Post a bet — The Action

Call a head-to-head against anyone in the event — low score over your chosen holes wins, and it settles itself from the scores.

1

Set it up

Open Action, pick the round / net or gross / holes / stake / opponent.

2

Choose who can see it, then post

Entire event (public board) or Just stakeholders (private). Tap Post bet — it goes live right away.

That's it

Score it. Watch it. Settle it.

No "what'd you make on 3?" after the round. No napkin math at the bar. The phone keeps the whole weekend.

Get on the app

Open it in your phone browser and add it to your home screen so it opens like an app.
