01 — Score Entry
⛳
Getting Started
Entering Your Scores

This is the screen you'll use every Friday. Open the app, join your group, and enter scores hole by hole as you play.

  1. Open wolf.dagle.cloud on your phone and tap the Score tab at the bottom.
  2. You'll see today's round listed. Tap it to select it.
  3. Enter your entry code — it's the year (e.g. 2026). This connects you to your group.
  4. You'll land on Hole 1. Enter each player's gross score (actual strokes). Net scores and stableford points are calculated automatically from handicaps.
  5. On par 3s, you'll see a toggle to mark greenies (hit the green and make par) and polies (make a putt longer than the flagstick pole).
  6. Use the arrow buttons to move between holes, or tap the hole number bar to jump to any hole.

💡 Scores save automatically

You can close the app and come back any time — your session is remembered. If you lose cell service on the course, scores are queued offline and sync when you're back online.

02 — The Board
🏆
Your Home Screen
The Board

The Board is the first thing you see when you open the app. It shows every round this season — upcoming, in-progress, and finished.

  1. Each round card shows the date, round number, and status (scheduled, active, or finalized).
  2. Tap a finished round to expand it and see the full scorecard for each group.
  3. The expanded view shows each player's stableford points, money won or lost, and who was wolf on each hole.
  4. Swipe between rounds or use the arrow buttons to browse the season.

📱 Add to your home screen

For the best experience, add the app to your phone's home screen. On iPhone: tap the share button → "Add to Home Screen." On Android: tap the menu → "Add to Home Screen." It'll look and feel like a real app.

03 — Wolf & Skins
🐺
The Game
Wolf & Skins

The app tracks the wolf game automatically. Here's how it works so you know what's happening on screen.

  1. Holes 1 & 2 are always skins — every player for themselves. Lowest net score wins.
  2. Starting on hole 3, the batting order (set during ball draw) determines who is wolf each hole. Player 1 is wolf on hole 3, player 2 on hole 4, and so on — cycling through the group.
  3. On wolf holes, the wolf watches each player tee off in order. After seeing the shots, the wolf either picks a partner (2v2) or goes lone wolf (1v3).
  4. Going lone wolf is riskier — if you win, each of the 3 opponents pays you (nearly 3× the reward). If you lose, you pay each of them.
  5. The admin records wolf decisions in the app. You just play golf and enter scores!
🎯

2v2 (Partner)

Wolf picks a partner. Best net ball of each team wins the hole.

🐺

Lone Wolf

Wolf goes alone against the other 3. Higher risk, double reward.

04 — Money
💰
The Stakes
How Money Works

The app calculates all money automatically — just enter the scores and it handles the rest. Here's the breakdown.

Situation What Happens
Wolf 2v2 (partner) Winning team collects from losing team
Lone Wolf wins Wolf collects from all 3 opponents (nearly 3×)
Lone Wolf loses Wolf pays all 3 opponents
Skins (holes 1-2) Lowest net wins, collects from everyone
Greenie (par 3) Hit the green in reg — bonus from each player
Polie (par 3) Putt longer than the flagstick pole — extra bonus

🧮 It's all automatic

You never have to do math. The app tracks every dollar won and lost per hole, per round, and for the season. Check the round detail or your player stats to see where you stand.

05 — Standings
📊
The Season
Standings & Harvey Cup

Track your position in the league all season long. Two competitions run simultaneously — and you can win both.

🏆

Wolf Cup

Based on stableford points. Your best 10 of 20 rounds count. Play well relative to your handicap and rack up points.

🎩

Harvey Cup

Based on head-to-head money results within your group each round. Beat the other guys, earn Harvey points.

  1. Tap the Standings tab to see the current season leaderboard for both competitions.
  2. Your best 10 of 20 rounds count for the Wolf Cup — so a bad week won't sink you.
  3. The Harvey Cup ranks you by cumulative head-to-head performance all season.
  4. Tap "History" at the top to see past champions and final standings from previous seasons.

🏅 Two trophies, one season

The Wolf Cup champion is the best overall player. The Harvey Cup champion is the best competitor in head-to-head matchups. You can win one, both, or neither — they're independent.

06 — Stats & Awards
📈
Your Legacy
Stats & Awards

The Stats page is where you see how everyone stacks up — and the awards showcase celebrates the league's greatest moments.

  1. Tap the Stats tab to see league-wide averages, money leaders, and the awards showcase.
  2. Tap any player's name to open their drill-down — a deep look at their performance.
  3. The drill-down shows their highlight reel (birdies, eagles, greenies, polies), partner chemistry, and batting order performance.
  4. The awards showcase displays trophies and badges earned across all seasons — past champions, iron man streaks, and more.

Quick Reference
⚡
Cheat Sheet
Quick Reference

Everything you need at a glance. Keep this page handy for your first few rounds.

📱 Getting to the App

Open wolf.dagle.cloud in your phone's browser. Add it to your home screen for quick access — it works like a regular app.

⛳ On the Course

Tap Score → select today's round → enter the year (e.g. 2026). Enter gross scores for each player on each hole. Mark greenies and polies on par 3s. That's it!

📶 Lost Signal?

No problem — scores queue locally and sync when you're back online. You'll see a red "Offline" indicator in the header.

🌙 Dark Mode

Tap the moon/sun icon in the top-right corner to switch between light and dark mode. Great for bright days on the course.

❓ Need Help?

Tap the help icon (circle with a question mark) in the header anytime to open the in-app guide.

✏️ Made a Mistake?

Go back to any hole and re-enter the score while the round is active. After finalization, the admin can make corrections from the admin panel.
