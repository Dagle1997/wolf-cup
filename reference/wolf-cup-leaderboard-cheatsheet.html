6 · Wolf Row — What Each Letter Means

W
Lone Wolf — you went 1v3 after seeing tee shots. Triple reward / triple loss.
B
Blind Wolf — you went 1v3 before tee shots. Extra multiplier.
JS
Green initials = your teammate (you're on their team for this 2v2).
KB
Red initials = opponent teammate (they're paired against you).
3v1
3v1 — wolf went alone and you're one of the 3 opponents.

7 · Side Game, Highlights & Quick Rules

🏆  Side Game Winner: Josh Stoll (Most Skins — 4)
After finalization the side-game winner banner shows above the table. During live play, the panel shows the week's side-game name and format.
  • • Holes 1 & 3 are always skins. No wolf, no polie/sandie payout (but still tracked for stats).
  • • Every other hole rotates the wolf by batting order.
  • • Greenies pay only on par 3s where the wolfer hits green and makes par.
  • • Polie = any made putt longer than the flagstick. Sandie = 1 shot out of the bunker + 1 putt in.
