Game Day
Everything happens on your phone
From lineup to final standings, the entire round flows through the app. One scorer per group keeps things moving.

Roster & Handicaps

The admin sets the week's lineup and the app pulls current GHIN handicap indexes automatically. Course handicaps calculate for the tee of the week — no more guessing or getting it wrong like game book.

  • Weekly attendance with live headcount
  • GHIN handicap lookup built in
  • Accurate course handicaps every week, every tee
  • Weighted pairing — play with different people each week
  • Optional digital ball draw — randomize order on your phone or toss balls like always

Hole-by-Hole Scoring

One person in each group enters scores after every hole. Wolf decisions, partner picks, greenies, and polies — all captured in seconds. The math happens instantly.

  • Gross scores → net, stableford, and money auto-calculated
  • Wolf / Partner / Blind Wolf decision flow
  • Greenie & polie bonus tracking per hole
  • Works offline — syncs when signal returns

Live Leaderboard

Everyone in the league can follow along in real time. See who's up, who's bleeding money, and tap any player to see their full scorecard mid-round.

  • Updates as each hole is saved
  • To par, stableford, money, and Harvey points
  • Expandable scorecards with wolf hole markers
  • Accessible to all league members — no login needed
The Race
Season standings, always current
The Wolf Cup race updates automatically after every finalized round. No more waiting for the spreadsheet.
  • Best 10 of 20 rounds count toward your season total
  • Combined stableford + money Harvey points per round
  • Drop-score logic applied automatically
  • Playoff rounds with weighted multipliers (R8 = 3x, R4 = 8x)
  • No more waiting for the official spreadsheet — it's live
Beyond Game Day
Stats that tell the real story

Deep Player Profiles

Tap any player to see their full story — wolf record, birdies, eagles, greenies, biggest wins, and worst losses. The Best Partnership banner highlights the league's most dominant duo.

  • Wolf call record (alone + blind wolf win %)
  • Birdies, eagles, greenies, polies season totals
  • Biggest round win & loss
  • Championship trophies & league badges
  • Best partnership spotlight at the top

Chemistry & Rivalries

Expand any player to see who they play best with — and who they should avoid. Hole-by-hole scoring averages, round history, and complete grouping money breakdowns.

  • Best & worst partner by 2v2 win rate
  • Lucky charm & rival callouts
  • Scorecard-style hole averages (front 9 / back 9)
  • Full money breakdown when grouped with anyone

Champions & Awards

Every season's champion is enshrined. The awards wall tracks Hall of Fame honors, superlatives, and bragging rights that the spreadsheet never captured.

  • Trophy gallery with all past champions
  • Dynasty, Back-to-Back, OG, and more awards
  • Champions & top-4 history back to 2015
How It Works
Four steps. Every week.
The scorer in each group handles entry. Everyone else just plays golf and watches the leaderboard.
1

Weekly Lineup

Admin confirms who's playing. The app shows the roster, current handicaps, and headcount for group planning.

2

Ball Draw & Groups

Toss balls like always, or use the app to draw and randomize — it's your call. Either way, enter the order and wolf assignments for all 18 holes appear instantly.

3

Score Each Hole

Enter 4 gross scores, pick wolf/partner/blind wolf, mark greenies and polies. Takes 15 seconds.

4

See Results Instantly

Net scores, stableford, money, and Harvey points calculate automatically. Standings update in real time.
