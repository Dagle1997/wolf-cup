New this week
The app now sets the line
Open the Scouting tab and it leads with The Line — a full sportsbook board pricing every member's odds to win the week.

Odds to win the week

Every member gets a real American price to post the most Harvey points that Friday — favorites in the black, live dogs in the middle, longshots out to +2500. Tap any name to drill in: your true win %, your confidence, and the reasoning behind the number.

  • Favorites → longshots — a board with real texture
  • Tap for the why — your Stableford + money rank, in plain English
  • Frozen before tee time — it can never peek at the result
  • Graded weekly — ✅ Chalk, 🎲 Upset, or 💥 Busted at the top of the board
  • Scrub the week selector to relive any past Friday's line
New this week
Know the foursome before you tee off
Below the line, Group Scouting breaks down this week's foursomes — the pre-round intel that used to live only in your head.

Scouting, by the group

Tap any player in your foursome to see exactly how they're playing — handicap trend, the holes they own, the holes they dread, and their average score on every hole, front nine and back.

  • 📈 Handicap trend — who's heating up, who's cooling off
  • 🎯 Best & worst holes, plus their go-to birdie hole
  • 🟦 Hole-by-hole scoring averages (green = under par)
  • ⚔️ Rivalries — who owns who in the money
  • 🤝 Lucky charms — the partnerships that just work
Also new this season
A season of upgrades
The app keeps getting sharper between Fridays. Plenty has landed since launch that we never made noise about:
🏆 Standings, redesigned — leader hero, the cut line, and a "pin me" to find yourself fast.
🔢 Fix the batting order — the scorer can correct a mis-entered draw mid-round, safely.
👥 Smoother start — pick your group before the draw, with "wrong group?" and auto-resume escape hatches.
📡 Live side-game leader — see who's winning the side game while the round is still going.
↕️ Sort the live board — by Harvey points, Stableford, or money.
🎯 Closest-to-pin, per par 3 — captured in-play on CTP weeks, not reconstructed later.
✨ Season Highlights reel — a rotating award widget: Birdies, Par 3 Champ, Sandman, Putting Master…
📊 Sharper stats — chemistry, rivalries (meet your Nemesis), and batting, all "higher = better."
🕳️ Hole-by-hole on the card — every player's per-hole scoring averages, one tap away.
🤝 Pairing transparency — public pairing history and who you've teamed with all season.
💰 Skins, listed — every skin winner shown, not just one.
🎲 Fairer ball draw — now a true random shuffle.
The Fine Print
Read it — it's funny
The Line is 100% for entertainment. No money changes hands, no accounts, no wallet — just the bragging rights of being the week's chalk (or the longshot who ruined everyone's card).

What the line can — and can't — price

✓Current form— beating your index
✓The money half— who owns wolf
✗Sandbagging— gaming your index
✗Dumping a round— tanking on purpose
✗Lightning— catching fire late
✓Handicaps equalize— so the edge stays honest

That last column is exactly why we still play it on grass instead of a spreadsheet.
